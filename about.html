About Us

Analytical & Computational Engineering (A&CE) was established in 2010 to provide data-driven engineering services.

A&CE solves technical problems as algorithm or library solutions driven by abstracted data. A&CE helps drive futuristic algorithm solutions to help utilize machine learning and artificial intelligence for engineering systems. These algorithm solutions running in compute engines can be utilized by state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems to drive insights and further automate engineering systems.

The typical use cases are:

  • Reusable engineering algorithms.
  • Algorithms/library delivered as a service.
  • Compute engine algorithms as web services.
