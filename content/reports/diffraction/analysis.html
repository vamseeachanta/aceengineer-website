Interpretation notes

  • Damping treatment matters for roll: a series with ζ = 0 % carries no external/viscous roll damping — its roll-resonance peak is an undamped potential-flow upper bound, not an operability prediction. Apply an external roll damping model before operational use.
  • Frequency-band scope: some series are published on a focused band (e.g. refined around the roll-response band); absence of rows outside the band is by scope, not omission.
  • Reliability flags travel with the data: rows flagged non-ok (e.g. undamped_resonance, low_freq_artifact) should be excluded from downstream use; definitions are on the dataset card.
  • Phase conventions: published phases use the Orcina lag convention (the export pipeline normalizes) — verify before phase-level cross-tool comparison.
  • See the dataset card for per-vessel data-quality notes and withheld tables. Cross-solver benchmarking lives in the comparison report.
