Comparison method

Magnitudes only by default. AQWA and OrcaWave publish phases in different conventions (OrcaWave: Orcina lag) — phase comparison requires an explicit convention alignment and is out of scope for the automatic statistics below.

Delta statistics are computed on the overlapping period band of the two series: series B is linearly interpolated in period onto series A's grid at each common heading. Peak deltas compare each series' own peak within the overlap band. Percentage deltas are reported relative to series A and are suppressed where |A| < 5 % of A's peak (to avoid noise-dominated ratios near zero crossings).

A physically meaningful roll comparison also requires matching damping treatment — ζ values for each series are shown in the availability table above; a mismatch is banner-flagged.
