04 · Interpretation boundary

What the evidence does—and does not—support

Supported

Frequency agreement, period-dependent forced-roll response, normalized response comparisons, aggregate loads, and the published numerical QA disposition.

Not yet supported

Full-cycle field animation, sectional tank-wall loads, private geometry reconstruction, or a vessel-level anti-roll benefit claim.

Next decision-grade step
