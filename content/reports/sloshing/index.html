Evidence maturity

Media depth and engineering depth are reported separately

A video makes a run easier to inspect; it does not by itself make the engineering record complete. Every browser card therefore shows both the artifact-based evidence tier and the available visual media. Representative and generic visuals are never presented as selected-case CFD evidence.

Reviewed publication boundary

Reports render from the immutable, content-addressed website release derived from aceengineer/digitalmodel-sloshing. Public records are source-neutral and geometry disclosure remains bounded.
