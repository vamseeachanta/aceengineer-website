05 · Interpretation and limits

Engineering use

Supported

First-mode frequency agreement, period-dependent forced-roll response, normalized cross-case comparisons, aggregate loads, and published numerical QA.

Qualified

The retained detailed case completed and was cycle-converged, but exceeded its configured Courant target. The exception remains visible in the QA report.

Not established

Sectional wall loads, full-cycle field animation, private geometry reconstruction, and vessel-level anti-roll benefit are not claims of this release.

Practical consequence. Use the period sweep to identify the strongest published loading region and the validation fixture to qualify frequency prediction. Do not treat the current aggregate-wall, two-dimensional results as a final design-load or vessel-performance assessment.
