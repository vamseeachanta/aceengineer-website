Subsea, Pipelines & Integrity

From line sizing to end-of-life integrity — ask in plain language and Deckhand runs the real calculation against DNV, ASME and PD codes, with every assumption traced.

Try it on Open Deck See the proof

What you can ask it to do

  • Wall thickness. Screen a pipeline wall across the design codes (DNV-ST-F101, ASME B31, PD 8010) and get a one-page report.
  • On-bottom stability. Check stability of a subsea line in waves and current (DNV-RP-F109).
  • Free-span / VIV. Screen a free span for vortex-induced vibration and allowable length (DNV-RP-F105).
  • Lateral & upheaval buckling. Check a line for snake buckling under pressure and temperature, and the cover a buried line needs.
  • Fitness-for-service. Assess a corroded line from wall-thickness readings against its design pressure.
  • Cathodic protection. Size sacrificial-anode protection for a pipeline, jacket, manifold, monopile or FPSO hull (DNV-RP-B401 / F103).
