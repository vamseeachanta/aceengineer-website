Wells & Subsurface

From decline curves to field economics — ask in plain language and Deckhand runs the analysis on public production data and returns a deliverable you can defend.

Try it on Open Deck Energy data

What you can ask it to do

  • Decline & EUR. Forecast a well's decline and estimate ultimate recovery (Arps).
  • Field economics. Run a field-development case — net present value, rate of return, payback — and a price / rate sensitivity.
  • Nodal analysis. Find a well's operating point from inflow and outflow.
  • Rod-pump diagnostics. Diagnose a sucker-rod pump from its dynamometer card and get a report.
  • Production summaries. Summarise lease, well and field production from public data (BSEE, Texas RRC, SODIR), and compare wells across a block.
