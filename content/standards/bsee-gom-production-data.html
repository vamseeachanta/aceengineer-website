Questions

What is the BSEE data?

The US Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement publishes Gulf of Mexico lease and well production data; Deckhand summarises and compares it without you wrangling the raw files.

What can I do with it?

Summarise a lease or well, compare wells across a block, and feed the history into a decline forecast or a field-economics case.
