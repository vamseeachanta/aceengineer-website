Questions

What does a dynacard show?

The load on the rod string through a pump stroke; its shape reveals pump conditions — gas interference, fluid pound, a worn or stuck pump — for a sucker-rod (beam) pumped well.

What does Deckhand return?

A reading of the card with the likely pump condition flagged and a short diagnosis report.
