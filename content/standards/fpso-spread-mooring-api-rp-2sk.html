Questions

What does API RP 2SK cover?

The design and analysis of station-keeping (mooring) systems for floating structures — line tensions, safety factors, and the intact and damaged conditions.

What can Deckhand return?

Line tensions and safety factors for a spread-mooring layout, the static equilibrium under environmental load, and the governing line and its utilisation.
