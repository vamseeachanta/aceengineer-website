Questions

What is a diffraction analysis for?

It computes how a floating hull responds to waves — added mass, damping, wave loads and the motion RAOs — the foundation for mooring, seakeeping and operability work.

Which tools does Deckhand prepare?

It sets up the input for boundary-element diffraction solvers (OrcaWave or AQWA) and checks the resulting coefficients and RAOs.
