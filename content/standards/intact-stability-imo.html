Questions

What governs intact stability?

The IMO Intact Stability (IS) Code criteria — minimum GM, righting-arm area ratios and a wind-heel check — adapted for offshore floaters and MODUs.

What does Deckhand return?

GM, the GZ curve, the wind-heel and area-ratio checks, and the governing criterion with its margin.
