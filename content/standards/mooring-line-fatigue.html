Questions

How is mooring fatigue estimated?

From the line's tension-cycle history and the component T-N (or S-N) data, accumulating damage by Miner's rule against the design service life. Deckhand reports the damage, the implied life and the governing line.

Which codes apply?

Offshore station-keeping codes — API RP 2SK and DNV-OS-E301 — govern mooring strength and fatigue; the component fatigue data depends on chain, wire or connector type.
