Questions

What is a riser combined-loading check?

A check that a riser's combined effective tension and bending stays within the allowable — by the API RP 2RD working-stress method or the DNV-OS-F201 load-and-resistance-factor (LRFD) method.

Which method should I use?

API RP 2RD is the working-stress approach; DNV-OS-F201 is the LRFD approach. Deckhand runs either and reports the governing load case and the utilisation.
