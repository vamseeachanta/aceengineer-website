Questions

Why are fibre-rope moorings checked differently?

Polyester rope has non-linear, time-dependent stiffness and creep, so service life is governed by fatigue, creep and minimum-tension limits rather than a single break-load check. Guidance such as API RP 2SM applies.

What does Deckhand report?

A service-life screen with the governing mode — fatigue, creep or minimum tension — and the assumptions on rope stiffness bounds.
