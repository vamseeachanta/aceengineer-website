Questions

What does a seakeeping screen tell me?

How a vessel moves in a sea state — motions, accelerations and natural periods — and whether motion comfort and operability limits are met for the operation.

What inputs are needed?

The vessel's response amplitude operators (RAOs) and the sea state (wave height, period, spectrum). Deckhand can also prepare the hydrodynamic model that produces the RAOs.
