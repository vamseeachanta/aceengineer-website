Questions

What is nodal analysis?

A method that finds a well's producing rate by intersecting its inflow performance (reservoir into wellbore) with its outflow performance (wellbore to surface) at a chosen node.

What does Deckhand return?

The IPR and VLP curves, the operating point, and how it shifts when a design parameter changes.
