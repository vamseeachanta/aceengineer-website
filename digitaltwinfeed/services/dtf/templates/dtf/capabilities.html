Capabilities

The analytical data are provided as a web service (data as service) with json results for further end use. These results can be of further use for engineering analysis, digital twin analysis and other third party verification purposes. The objective of the DigitalTwinFeed portal is to keep the results in a state of readiness and ready to use for making daily status, operational and strategic use.

The engineering data provided will comply with the industry codes and standards where applicable. Additionally, all data is provided with data context, quality checks (if applicable) and historical data (if available) to quickly relate and assimilate the data.


Energy Engineering

Onshore and Offshore Energy engineering consisting of renewables (wind and wave) and oil and gas.

The Shallow Water Offshore Analysis comprises of following:
  • Foundation strength and stability for structures
  • Fatigue analysis and foundation centralizer placement & centralizer gap optimization
  • Platform Centralizer Interface design
  • Support energy related drilling, production and workover operations
  • Analysis performed per guidelines of Institute of Petroleum (IP Guidelines) for fixed platform well riser systems

    • Structural Design and Dynamic Analysis

  • Wall thickness sizing per API, DNV and 30 CFR 250 requirements

  • Steel catenary risers (SCR)
  • Flexible catenary risers (FCR)
  • Top tensioned risers (TTR)
  • Free standing hybrid risers (FSHR)
  • Single line offset risers (SLOR)
  • Wall thickness sizing
  • High pressure and high temperature (HPHT) applications
  • Riser analysis and design verification performed per API-RP-2RD, API-STD-2RD and 30 CFR 250 code of federal regulations
    • Subsea structural strength/extreme analysis as follows:
  • Riser configuration optimization and tension requirements

  • Drift-off/drive-off
  • Watch circles
  • Weak point analysis
