Introduction

DigitalTwinFeed provides consistent and reliable analytical data assessments and insights for digital twins. A typical industrial asset digital twin helps in design, maintenance and integrity assessments. These assessments are provided as data services to assist asset owners make timely operational and economic decisions. The asset design and measured data is used to perform advanced engineering analysis to provide timely insights for digital twins in a software/data as a service (SAS) model.



The company is founded in 2013 to help clients adapt digitally and also help transform the Industrial world. With over 100 years of experience equally in engineering and programming, the DigitalTwinFeed provides services for digital transition for Industry 4.0 (IR4.0). The digital insights, tools and data is provided by fundamental engineering and programming. The engineering is driven by fundamentals and quality. The software architecture and programming are driven by test driven development (TDD) to ensure data services quality and insight consistency. The services are Serverless utilizing Functions as a Service (FaaS) architecture. The services leverage cloud infrastructure and platform for compute, storage and network.

DigitalTwinFeed implements data driven engineering (DDE) to help minimize repeat and mundane work from engineer's day to day life. All data services are provided with a methodology for technical reference. This methodology also serves as data audit reference.

The mission of the DigitalTwinFeed is to enable client's success by providing digital insights, tools and data. DigitalTwinFeed objective is to feed analytical asset information utilizing advanced engineering.
